# M&A Cyber Risk Committee - Governance Charter

## Purpose
Establish governance for cyber risk decisions during M&A activities.

## Proposed Committee
- Chair: Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)
- Members: Head of IT, Head of Legal, Head of Risk, M&A lead, Head of Operations

## Responsibilities
- Approve risk appetite for acquisitions
- Review due diligence findings and classify targets
- Make integration decisions and sign-off remediation plans

## RACI (sample)
- Identify issues: Risk Team (R), Target IT (A)
- Remediate issues: Target IT (R), Acquirer IT (C), CISO (A)
