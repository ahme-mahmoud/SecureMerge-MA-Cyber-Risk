M&A Cyber Evidence Checklist

This checklist supports due diligence by identifying what documents, technical artifacts and validations must be collected from the target company.

1. Policy & Governance Documents

Evidence ItemDescriptionStatus
Information Security PolicyHigh-level policy for security controlsRequired
Access Control PolicyRules for accounts, privileges and MFARequired
Incident Response PlanDefined IR roles, procedures and playbooksRequired
Business Continuity PlanDisaster recovery and resilience planRequired
Third-Party Risk PolicyHow vendors and SaaS are approvedRequired

2. Technical Architecture Evidence

Evidence ItemDescriptionStatus
Network DiagramsLAN/WAN, segmentation, firewall topologyRequired
Application ArchitectureKey business systems, integrations & APIsRequired
Cloud ArchitectureAWS / Azure / GCP diagrams, IAM designRequired
Data Flow MapsSensitive data paths and storage locationsRequired

3. Security Controls Evidence

Evidence ItemDescriptionStatus
Vulnerability Scan ReportsRecent authenticated scans (Nessus/Qualys)Required
Penetration Test ReportsInternal + external pen test resultsRequired
Logging & SIEM CoverageLogging matrix + SIEM dashboardsRequired
Backup VerificationEvidence of tested backup restoreRequired

4. Compliance & Legal Evidence

Evidence ItemDescriptionStatus
SOC 2 / ISO 27001 CertificatesCompliance documentationIf available
Data Processing AgreementsGDPR / local compliance evidenceRequired
Licensing & Software OwnershipLicense compliance proofRequired
Vendor Risk AssessmentsSecurity evaluation for key suppliersRequired

5. HR & Security Culture Evidence

Evidence ItemDescriptionStatus
Training RecordsCybersecurity awareness attendanceRequired
Privileged Users ListAdmins, power users, service accountsRequired
Termination Access LogsHow quickly accounts are disabledRequired
