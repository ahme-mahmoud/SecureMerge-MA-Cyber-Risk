M&A Cyber Governance Charter

This governance charter defines decision-making structure, roles, responsibilities and reporting for cyber due diligence and integration activities during M&A acquisitions.

1. M&A Cyber Risk Committee

  • CISO â€“ Committee Chair
  • Head of Governance, Risk & Compliance
  • IT Infrastructure Lead
  • Security Operations Lead
  • Legal & Compliance Representative
  • Acquisition Project Manager

2. Committee Responsibilities

  • Review cyber due diligence results
  • Approve risk scoring and classification
  • Determine integration strategy (full / segmented / ring-fenced)
  • Approve temporary exceptions and compensating controls
  • Oversee remediation roadmap for inherited risks
  • Track progress and report to executive leadership

3. RACI Matrix (Roles & Accountability)

Activity CISO GRC Infra SOC Legal PM
Cyber Due Diligence Review ARCCCI
Risk Scoring Approval ARIIII
Integration Strategy Decision ACRCCI
Exception Handling ARCCRI
Remediation Tracking CRIIIA

4. Executive Reporting Pack

  • M&A Cyber Risk Summary Dashboard
  • Top 10 Inherited Risks
  • Integration Readiness Score
  • Remediation Progress Chart (Weekly)
  • Exception Register (Approved / Pending / Expired)
