# M&A Cyber Due Diligence Playbook

## Purpose
A practical playbook to guide cyber due diligence during Mergers & Acquisitions. It contains the questionnaire, evidence checklist and scoring guidance.

## Sections
1. Executive summary
2. Questionnaire (items and weights)
3. Evidence checklist (policies, network diagrams, vuln scans, pen test reports, audit findings)
4. Scoring methodology and thresholds
5. Integration recommendations
6. Remediation roadmap template

## Questionnaire (sample)
- Systems: Unsupported OS (weight 2)
- Systems: Patch cadence (weight 1)
- Identity: Centralized IDM (weight 1)
- Identity: MFA (weight 2)
- ...

## Evidence checklist (sample)
- Information Security Policy
- Network diagrams (logical, seg, DMZ)
- Vulnerability scans and reports
- Penetration test reports
- Recent audit findings and remediation logs

## Scoring
- Convert answers to numeric weights, sum per category and overall.
- Classification: Low / Medium / High (configurable thresholds)

## Deliverable
Use this playbook to capture findings, populate the risk register and produce the executive reporting pack.
